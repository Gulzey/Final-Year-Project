About Us

CycleNav is the ultimate cycling map crafted by Gulied Mohamoud Abdi, a final year Software Engineering student at The University of the West of England. This website is designed to provide both novice and experienced cyclists with the most detailed and user-friendly cycle maps. Our goal is to enhance your cycling experience, ensuring you find not only the safest routes but also the most scenic paths your city has to offer.

Our maps are developed using cutting-edge GIS technology and data collected from numerous cycling enthusiasts to ensure accuracy and reliability. Whether you're commuting to work, exploring new trails, or planning a leisurely ride across town, our platform is your go-to guide for all things cycling.

Join our community today and turn every cycling journey into an adventure!

Gulied Mohamoud Abdi

CEO and Lead Developer of CycleNav
